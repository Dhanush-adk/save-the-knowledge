# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Help keep discussions technical and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues/PRs/discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.

Severe or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent participation bans.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness misconduct, contact project maintainers privately with context and links/screenshots when possible.
